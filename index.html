Built For Arizona Sun

Ceramic-Grade
Protection

Phoenix heat and UV are brutal on paint. Our ceramic coating creates a hydrophobic, chemical-resistant layer that shields your car's finish and keeps that just-detailed shine for years, not weeks.

  • Hydrophobic Shine

    Water and dirt bead off the surface instead of sticking, so every future wash is faster and easier.

  • UV & Swirl Protection

    A sacrificial layer that absorbs sun exposure and wash-induced swirls, so your original paint stays untouched.

  • Multi-Year Durability

    Backed by our workmanship guarantee, built to outlast a standard wax by years, not weeks.
